Yue Zhang

Research Scientist @ Meta | GenAI

I am a Research Scientist at Meta, specializing in Large Language Model (LLM) post-training and AI agent. Prior to joining Meta, I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from UC Merced, focusing on machine learning-augmented ubiquitous computing systems. I hold M.S. and B.S. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University. Please feel free to contact me via email (yzhang58 at ucmerced dot edu) if you want to connect.
